ABOUT ME

MARYNA ZHURAVLOVA

Nice to meet you! My name is Maryna Zhuravlova. I am a student of Global IT at Seifu Institute of Information Science. At university, I studied to become a Japanese translator and teacher. At lessons I was translating texts in various fields, including IT. At that time, I understood that I liked the IT field and wanted to learn more about it. So after graduating from the university, I went to Japan to study IT. At japanese school, I am learning EXCEL, POWER POINT, PYTHON, HTML & CSS, etc. After studying for a year, I become interested in Web Design. When I use Japanese sites, I often feel that they are difficult to read. So, I wanted to make the Japanese site easier for users to use. In the future, I would like to design and develop websites for small businesses to large corporations.
